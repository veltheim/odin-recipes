Currywurst

Ingredients

  • 500g passata (jar)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tbsp balsamic or red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 dashes of Tabasco or cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Bratwurst sausages
  • Sunflower or rapeseed oil

Steps

  1. This dough is enough for about 20 cookies.
  2. Place all the sauce ingredients in a saucepan and simmer over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Cut the sausages approximately 1-2 cm wide with a knife. Heat oil in a pan and fry the sausages over medium heat for approximately 10 minutes, turning several times.
  4. Serve the sausages with the sauce and a sprinkle of curry powder.
